Compost Life

below is a list of life seen inside the compost bin at some time or other since we first started using it
  • Earth worms - Addmittedly put there by me when digging up some garden
  • Fruit flies
  • Little black flies - look like storm flies
  • Green bottle flies
  • Spiders - differnt breeds but I have not identified the different types
  • Slugs
  • Snails
  • Wasp's - they like feeding on the waste fruit
  • Potato - Yes we have had a potato growing through the heap
  • Mould
  • Beetles
  • A Moth
