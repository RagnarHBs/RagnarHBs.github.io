Thevenin's theorem

a) Divide the circuit being assed into two at a convenient point
b) Calculated / find the open circuit voltage across the two terminals at the divide point
c) Calculate / find the impedance across the two points having first short circuit the voltage sources
d) Create the equivalent circuit using a single voltage source and equivalent impedance

Superposition theorem

1) Can be applied to any linear circuit
2) When there are independent sources, calculate the voltage and currents for each source independently
3) Add the results algebraically
